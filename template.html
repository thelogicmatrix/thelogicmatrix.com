Layering Realities

a Field Guide to the Royal Park

Core Question

What is the purpose and use case for parks in modernity and within urban environments?

Summary

This is an exploration of what purpose parks have in our modern society, as well as within our modern cities. The way I have decided to format this exploration of these concepts I have decided to put it to a collage of images, soundscapes and 3D models of the Royal Park. Through this and exploratory blog posts I will cover the concepts that I am trying to dig down into.
