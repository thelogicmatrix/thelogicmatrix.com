The Trees of Royal Park

Introduction

Here, I will go over a few of these trees that I managed to get nice large full shots of, as well as provide an expansive look at different trees and their barks.

Eucalyptus Tree

The Eucalyptus tree is an iconic Australian species, easily recognizable by its distinct bark and fragrant leaves. Famously, their leaves are eaten by koalas, however there were no koalas to be found in the park :(

Oak Tree

I attempted to make a 3D scan that you can find in the main page of an oak, it's really quite a large and impressive tree, originating from England. I saw one that was absolutely immense that I didn't quite get photos of because I got distracted by the smaller easier to photograph oak.
