Grass: The Whispers of the Wind

Introduction

Ok, I've got to admit, on it's face it does seem impossible to write and go at length about GRASS of all things but thinking about it, looked upon as a dichotomy of the natural and the urban it's pretty interesting. I will looking at grass through the lens of utility and looking into it's purpose.

The Urban Green

Like a military cut, the urban manicured lawns of most parks and sports areas are rather interesting, almost a green pavement. A surface manufactured. It's great for human, lovely to sit upon, great to footy on, all around great for humans. However, it does present as lacking and barren over that of the waving wild grasses.

Grass as it Grew

Wild grass is unmanaged and unfurled, free to move about as it wishes, definitely not ideal to picnic in, unless you're partial to enjoy addition green and perhaps crawlies with your sandwiches. But in this thick carpet, it invites small critters and a diversity of plants to germinate and thrive. As the wind blows over the long strands of blond grass, there is a peace to it.

Whispers of the Grass

I have decided to add an audio element to this, to attempt to capture the ambience of the grasses, as you move through them and the wind blows.
